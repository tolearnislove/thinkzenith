What are NFTs?
NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token — a unique digital item stored on a blockchain with a verifiable owner and full transaction history. No two NFTs are identical, giving them real market value as collectibles and tradeable assets.
What is the TON Blockchain?
TON (The Open Network) is a fast, low-fee blockchain built alongside Telegram's infrastructure. It powers Telegram's digital economy — from in-app payments using Stars to full ownership of Telegram-native NFT assets. Everything we trade lives on TON.
Telegram Username NFTs
Via Fragment.com, premium Telegram usernames and phone numbers can be minted as NFTs on TON. Once minted, they are permanently ownable and transferable. A short, desirable handle can be worth thousands of dollars on the open market.
Telegram Gift NFTs
Telegram introduced limited-edition digital gifts that users can convert into NFTs on the TON chain. Rare gifts from limited drops carry real collectible value and are actively traded worldwide in the secondary market.
What are Telegram Stars?
Telegram Stars is the official in-app currency of Telegram. Purchased with real money, Stars are used to send digital gifts, unlock features, and participate in the NFT economy inside Telegram.
Why use Escrow?
In P2P trading, either party can vanish after receiving funds or the asset. An escrow holds both sides simultaneously, verifies everything, then releases both at once. Neither buyer nor seller can lose out. This is how we operate on every deal.
